Thank you for participating in this experiment. Please read the following instructions carefully.

The results of our research and your payout depend on you understanding the experiment.

This experiment will have attention check questions. If you fail them, your payout will be reduced.

Otherwise, there are no right or wrong answers in this experiment and we treasure your honesty.

This experiment will consist of an entry survey, two tasks (task 1 and task 2) and an exit survey. Each task is followed by a short questionnaire. After the exit survey, you will be directed to a page where you can leave your bank details for the payout transfer. All experimental data is collected anonymously. Payout data is stored separately.

The experiment will take approximately 45 minutes and will be rewarded with a payout of €12 to €15 depending on your performance.

During most parts of this experiment, you will hear background music. Pages which should play music are marked with a blue badge at the top right corner of the screen. If you see the badge but do not hear music at any point during the experiment, please raise your hand. The experimenter will come and assist you.
