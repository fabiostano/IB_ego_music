In the following task, you will presented with two products for each question.

Your task is to decide which product you prefer between the two products. You will have to wait at least 5 seconds before making a choice. Please take the time to consider your options.

Sample Question:

You will be presented with a total of 67 questions. The number of questions completed will be indicated as a progress bar at the top of your screen.

To ensure that your choices reflect your preferences, 3 questions out of the 67 will be repeated at the end. You will have to recall at least 2 of the previous choices in order to receive the full payout. A bonus will be given if all 3 of the same choices are recalled. You will have a short questionnaire after this task.

2 12€
3 + 3€
