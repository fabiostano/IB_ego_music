The Task:

The Task: You will see an online store, where you get the opportunity to buy products. These products will be at a lower price compared to usual prices in a normal supermarket. (Discounts about 50%)

This task will consist of 4 rounds.

In each round, you can decide whether you want to buy one of the two products on offer. You have a limited amount of time to make your decision, which can be seen on the countdown timer at the top of the page.

Products will change from round to round and they do not repeat. Accordingly, you will have the opportunity to buy up to 4 different products. After each round, there will be some questions about your decision.

After the experiment is finished, one participant per session will be chosen at random to receive the products they bought in the experiment. If you decide to buy a product, the price of the discounted product will be subtracted from your current payout.

If you are the chosen participant, you will receive the products you bought after the experiment, and the remainder of your payout will be transferred to your bank account.

If you are not chosen, you will receive none of the products you bought, and your payout will be transferred to your bank account.

Example A:

Suppose your payout from the first task is 12,00€ and you decide to buy one product for 0,40€. After the experiment, you are chosen to receive your product.

You will receive your product right after the experiment. The payout transferred to your bank account will be 12,00€ – 0,40€ = 11,60€.

Example B:

Suppose your payout from the first task is 12,00€ and you decide to buy one product for 0,40€. After the experiment, you are not chosen to receive your product.

You will not receive the product after the experiment. The payout transferred to your bank account will be 12,00€
